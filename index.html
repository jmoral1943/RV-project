Aurora Borealis

The Northern Lights also called Aurora Borealis, and Norðurljós in Icelandic. The northern lights is one of the most spectualar events you can see on earth. The Northern Lights are a caused by solar particles ionising high in the earth's magnetic atomsphere. The lights appear like they are frolicking across the vase night sky. The colors are usually green, but could be purople, red, pink, orange, and even blue.
