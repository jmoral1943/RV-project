Blue Lagoon

The beautiful infamous Blue Lagoon is a freak of nature. In the land of ice to have this random hot spring is a nice supripse. Once you are there you'll have the chance to get a face mask made from the Blue Lagoon’s primary elements, used in tandem with silica and algae unlock the door to healthy, luminous, ageless skin. You can also either get a in-water massage, sauna & steam room , and the in-water bar. This is an experience that can't be missed out if you visit Iceland.

Price: Starting at $56

Age: 2+

Things Included:

  • Entrance to the Blue Lagoon
  • Silica mud mask
  • Use of towel
  • 1st drink of your choice

See Dettifoss Waterfall in North Iceland

The tour will start by leaving Lake Mývatn and drive towards Dettifoss Waterfall. From there you arrive at the one of the most powerful waterfall in Europe, 100m wide and with a fall of 44m. From there you will take a short walk from the parking lot to the best view of the waterfall. From there you can see the adjacent waterfalls of Hafragilsfoss and Selfoss. To make sure that this stays the best experience for everyone iff the ground is covered in snow, you should use a pair of the provided snowshoes to make the hike easier. We also provide some of the best local tour guides that will bring to the most stunning areas of the waterfalls.

Take a short break to get something to eat or drink at a local cafe or restaurant. Then head back to Lake Mývatn.

Price: Starting at $165.55

Age: 12+

Things Included:

  • Small-group guided tour
  • Transportation in a minibus
  • Hotel pick-up and drop-off
  • 1st drink of your choice
